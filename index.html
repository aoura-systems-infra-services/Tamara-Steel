About Us

Built on Trust,
Driven by Precision

Tamara Steel LLC was founded in 2023 as an independent entity, originating from Al Huraiz Steel, to take over its projects and operational activities. The company was established with a clear mission: to expand operations and meet the growing demands of the UAE's construction market.

From the outset, Tamara Steel quickly earned recognition and trust among major clients across the region. The same dedicated team that handled design, engineering, and project execution under Al Huraiz Steel continues these responsibilities under Tamara Steel — including fulfilling obligations of previously completed projects.

The company's strength lies in its deep design capabilities, engineering expertise, and a results-driven approach to every project — ensuring the highest standards of support for clients and contractors alike.
