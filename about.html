Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Precision

Tamara Steel LLC was founded in 2023 as an independent entity, originating from Al Huraiz Steel, to take over its projects and related activities. The company was created with a clear goal: to expand operations and meet the growing demands of the UAE's construction market.

From the outset, Tamara Steel quickly earned recognition and trust among major clients across the region — including top developers like EMAAR, DAMAC, Dubai Holdings, Nakheel, and Majid Al Futtaim Properties.

The company's strength lies in its deep design capabilities, engineering expertise, and a results-driven approach to every project. The same dedicated team that handled design, engineering, and project execution under Al Huraiz Steel continues these responsibilities under Tamara Steel — including fulfilling the obligations of previously completed projects.

In 2024, Tamara Steel expanded its operations with the opening of a new factory located in Sajjah Industrial Oasis, Sharjah — launched to further strengthen its structural fabrication capabilities and meet increasing market demand.
